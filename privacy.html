Privacy Policy

Tarik Auto-Poster — Effective date: 2025-12-25

This Privacy Policy explains how Tarik Auto-Poster ("we", "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our app and related services.

1. What the app is

Tarik Auto-Poster is an automation tool that helps users publish content to TikTok using TikTok’s APIs, only after the user authorizes access through TikTok’s consent flow.

2. Information we collect

Depending on how you use the app, we may process:

  • TikTok authorization data: access tokens / refresh tokens (if issued), scopes, and related identifiers required to call TikTok APIs.
  • Account/creator info (limited): basic identifiers returned by TikTok necessary to link your authorization to your account (e.g., user/creator id).
  • Content metadata you provide: title, description, hashtags, scheduling info.
  • Media you upload for posting: video files and optional thumbnails that you choose to publish.
  • Technical/log data: timestamps, request logs, error logs (to operate and secure the service).

3. How we use information

  • To authenticate you via TikTok and maintain your session.
  • To publish or schedule posts on your behalf based on your explicit actions/settings.
  • To troubleshoot errors, prevent abuse, and improve reliability.
  • To comply with legal requirements and enforce our Terms.

4. Storage and retention

  • Tokens and required identifiers are stored only as long as needed to provide the service or until you revoke access.
  • Uploaded media is stored only for the time needed to publish (and may be deleted after posting, unless you choose otherwise in your workflow).
  • Security and operational logs may be retained for a limited period for auditing and abuse prevention.

5. Sharing and third parties

  • TikTok: We share data with TikTok only as necessary to perform API actions you authorize (e.g., posting).
  • No selling: We do not sell your personal data.
  • No advertising data brokers: We do not share your data with data brokers.

6. Security

We apply reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect data (access control, least-privilege, and secure handling of tokens). However, no system is 100% secure.

7. Your choices

  • You can revoke TikTok authorization at any time from your TikTok account settings (where available) or by discontinuing use.
  • You can request deletion of stored data by contacting us (see Contact).

8. Children

This service is not intended for children and should not be used by anyone under the age required by TikTok’s terms.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. We will update the effective date at the top of this page.
